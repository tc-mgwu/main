Autumn performs in various venues throughout the Bay Area. Check this website for information on her past and upcoming performances.

Teaching

As a classically trained soprano, Autumn teaches private voice lessons in her Lucas Valley based studio. She welcomes students of all skill levels.

Conducting

Autumn is the Director of the Marin Men’s Chorus. She also works with choirs and choruses to enhance their knowledge of healthy vocal technique.

Artistic Philosophy

The purpose of singing is not only to create beautiful sound, but to also
connect with people in a way that invigorates, disturbs, saddens or excites.
It is my passion to use the voice to communicate deeply with the audience.

This is art: to create a moment in which the one observing sees oneself,
sees one's own exuberance, one's own sadness or turmoil. These
moments of expressivity and connection are what I live for, and
what I intend to help my students achieve.
