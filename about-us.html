About Autumn



Biography

From a young age, Autumn Allee had expressed an interest in theatre and vocal arts. As a soprano, she is praised for her unique instrument, as well as, her thoughtful acting and commanding stage presence. She has performed leading and supporting roles in productions at both of her alma maters, as well as, in young artists programs such as Kentucky Opera and BASOTI.


Education

M.M. Vocal Performance, University of Louisville

B.M. Vocal Performance, Lawrence Conservatory of Music
